Academics

Structured learning from foundation to college

Our academic programs are thoughtfully designed to support every stage of a child's learning journey, aligned with board requirements and enriched with skills for real life.

School Section

Playgroup to Matriculation (K–10)

We follow a concept-based approach where every lesson connects to practical understanding. Regular written work, spoken activities and formative assessments ensure a strong foundation for board classes.

English medium instruction Daily Nazra & Tajweed classes Activity-based learning in early years
  • Early Years Playgroup, Nursery, KG – joyful learning with phonics, rhymes and basic numeracy.
  • Primary Classes I–V with strong focus on English, Mathematics, Science and Islamic Studies.
  • Middle Classes VI–VIII, concept clarity, creative writing and exam techniques.
  • Matric (IX–X) Science & Arts groups with test sessions, past papers and board-oriented preparation.
Intermediate College

Pre-Medical, Pre-Engineering, ICS & I.Com

Our college section is led by experienced lecturers who focus on conceptual depth, exam strategies and career guidance to help students succeed in competitive entrance tests and university admissions.

Separate sections for boys & girls Regular test series Entry test preparation support
  • F.Sc Pre-Medical For aspiring doctors, pharmacists and life-science professionals.
  • F.Sc Pre-Engineering Strong mathematics and physics base for engineering careers.
  • ICS Computer science focus for IT, software and tech fields.
  • I.Com Business, accounting and commerce-oriented program.
